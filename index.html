NYTHRA

Nythra ye dikhai nahi deta, par woh tumhare andar rehta hai. Har us darr, har us cheekh, aur har us gehri iccha ke sath woh aur takatwar hota jata hai, jise tum chupani ki koshish karte ho uuse woh tumhare andar ke andhere ko jagaata hai.

POWERS

Powers ke baare me abhi nahi bataunga kyu ki kafi complicated hai, Surface level pe dekho toh fire, water, ice aur baki elements jaisi cheezein mil jayenge, and some special things like mainly toh weapons hote hai like swords and stuff, and a little bit of, yaar jane do ye tum khud dekh lena mujhe samjh nhi aa rha kyse batau.

ART PROCESS

Har character main khud draw karta hu.

And yah , MAIN AI USE KARTA HU. Lekin full manga banane ke liye nahi.

Pehle main story structure likhta hu.

Scenes plan karta hu. Rough sketches aur panels draw karta hu. Agar tum mere Instagram par jaoge toh unme se kuch dekh sakte ho. Uske baad main un rough panels aur character references ko AI ko dekh ke ek image create karwata hun. Fir unhe trace, edit aur improve karta hu. Details add karta hu. Aur final look fully different.

Isliye mere liye AI ek tool hai. Artist nahi. Brush hai. Painter nahi.

ABOUT

Atma Rekha is an original Indian manga/comic that combines mythology, mystery, action, supernatural fantasy and emotional storytelling. Inspired by Indian culture and long-form worldbuilding, the story explores themes of love, sacrifice, fear, destiny and the unknown.
