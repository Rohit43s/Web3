THE UNKNOWN

Kuch log unhe Narakshas kehte hain… aur kuch bache hi nahi ki unka naam rakh sakein. Is duniya mein alag-alag rakshas hain, sirf janwar nahi. bade aur bhayanak janwar, ya danav khe sakte hai, har din ek nayi kahani hoti hai ya to bachne ki… ya mit jaane ki.

NYTHRA

Nythra ye dikhai nahi deta, par woh tumhare andar rehta hai. Har us darr, har us cheekh, aur har us gehri iccha ke sath woh aur takatwar hota jata hai, jise tum chupani ki koshish karte ho woh tumhare andar uss andhere ko jagaata hai.

POWERS

Powers ke baare me abhi nahi bataunga kyu ki kafi complicated hai, Surface level pe dekho toh fire, water, ice aur baki elements jaisi cheezein mil jayenge, and some special things like mainly toh weapons hote hai like swords and stuff, and a little bit of, yaar jane do ye tum khud dekh lena mujhe samjh nhi aa rha kyse batau.

ART PROCESS

Har character main khud draw karta hu.

And yah i have USED AI in it.

Pehle main story structure likhta hu.

Scenes plan karta hu. Rough sketches aur panels draw karta hu. Uske baad main un rough panels aur character references ko AI ko dekh ke ek image create karwata hun. Fir unhe trace, edit aur improve karta hu. Details add karta hu.

so AI ek tool hai. Artist nahi. Brush hai. Painter nahi.

ABOUT

Atma Rekha is an original Indian manga/comic that combines mythology, mystery, action, supernatural fantasy and emotional storytelling. Inspired by Indian culture and long-form worldbuilding, the story explores themes of love, sacrifice, fear, destiny and the unknown.
