THE UNKNOWN

Kuch log unhe Narakshas kehte hain… aur kuch bache hi nahi ki unka naam rakh sakein. Is duniya mein alag-alag rakshas hain, sirf janwar nahi. Yeh bade aur bhayanak janwar, ya danav, hain jo insaanon ko khate hain. Insaan ab jee nahi raha… bas kisi tarah bach raha hai. Aur har din ek nayi kahani hoti hai ya to bachne ki… ya mit jaane ki.

NYTHRA

Nythra ye dikhai nahi deta, par woh tumhare andar rehta hai. Har us darr, har us cheekh, aur har us gehri iccha ke sath woh aur takatwar hota jati hai jise tum chupani ki koshish karte ho. woh tumhare andar ke andhere ko jagaata hai. Woh tumhari soch ko itna kar dega ki tum khud se darne lagoge. Woh humare kaam ko itna bhatka dega ki har raasta tumaru barbadi par khatam hoga.

POWERS

Powers ke baare me abhi zyada batana story ke liye thoda dangerous hoga 😭Surface level pe dekho toh fire, water, ice aur baki elements jaisi cheezein mil jayengi. Lekin Atma Rekha me powers sirf powers nahi hai. Har power ke piche ek reason hai. Ek source hai. Ek price hai. Aur kuch powers aysi hai jinke baare me unke users ko bhi puri sachai nahi pata. Jitni badi power... Utna bada uska asar. Aur kabhi kabhi... Power se zyada khatarnak woh cheez hoti hai jo usse dekh rahi hoti hai

YEH IDEA AAYA KAHA SE?

Honestly... Mujhe pata hai shayad tum is part me interested nahi hoge. Lekin phir bhi bata deta hu 😭 Ek din mujhe ek strange dream aaya. Aaj bhi mujhe uska exact scene yaad nahi. Bas us dream ka feeling yaad hai. Fir maine uske baare me baar baar sochna start kiya. Aur kuch time baad wahi type ke dreams mujhe lagbhag roz aane lage. Mostly final battle type scenes.

Destroyed places. Unknown faces. Aur bohot saare questions. Maine yeh sab apne friends ko bataya. Kuch ne bola "nice".

Kuch ne bola "bhai jaldi soya kar" 😭 Aur ek ne bola...

ART PROCESS

Har character main khud draw karta hu.

And yah , MAIN AI USE KARTA HU. Lekin full manga banane ke liye nahi.

Pehle main khud dialogues likhta hu.

Scenes plan karta hu. Rough sketches aur panels draw karta hu. Agar tum mere Instagram par jaoge toh unme se kuch dekh bhi sakte ho. Uske baad main un rough panels aur character references ko AI ke through visualize karta hu. Fir unhe trace, edit aur improve karta hu. Details add karta hu. Aur final look fully different.

Isliye mere liye AI ek tool hai. Artist nahi. Brush hai. Painter nahi.
